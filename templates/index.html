Simple Online Quizzes

You can create a quiz in minutes. Send a single link to all your students, grade their responses from your phone, and send the results back by email automatically.

How it works

This is a step by step description of what you and your students will see while making, taking, and grading a quiz.

Create the Quiz

Fill-in-the-blank questions have text before and after the blank. Create the question by filling in those text boxes.

Share with your Students

Once you're done, we'll give you a link. Send it to all your students. They'll see a form like this:
  1. I am going my cousin's birthday party.
After they take the quiz, they'll get a link to their results page, which will be initially blank.

Grade the Quiz

Once your students have taken the quiz, you'll see a form like this.
  1. I am going my cousin's birthday party.
    名前 答え 正解 不正解 正しい答え
    JT to
    Chisa at
